## 安装

Ubuntu安装

1. 在解压文件夹，执行```sh bin/pycharm.sh``` 弹出界面点击下一步

2. 快捷方式在/usr/share/applications 创建  pycharm.desktop 路径需要指定具体绝对路径

   ```desktop
   [Desktop Entry]
   Version=1.0
   Type=Application
   Name=Pycharm
   Icon=/home/mengdan/Downloads/pycharm-2018.2.1/bin/pycharm.png
   Exec=sh /home/mengdan/Downloads/pycharm-2018.2.1/bin/pycharm.sh
   MimeType=application/x-py;
   Name[en_US]=pycharm
   ```
